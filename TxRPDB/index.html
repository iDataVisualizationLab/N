Welcome to TxRPDB!

Texas Rigid Pavement Database (TxRPDB) aims to provide a comprehensive enterprise platform for sharing rigid pavement performance data in Texas. Currently, TxRPDB provides design, structural and performance data for continuously reinforced concrete pavement (CRCP) and jointed concrete pavement (CPCD) in Texas. Additionally, findings from TxDOT and national research projects and special and experimental sections built over the years by TxDOT can be accessed through TxRPDB. TxRPDB is a product of TxDOT Research Project 0-6274: “Project Level Performance Database for Rigid Pavements in Texas – II” and is being developed and managed by Texas Tech Center for Multidisciplinary Research in Transportation (TechMRT), Texas Tech University.

About Us.

TxRPDB currently comprises of the following types of Rigid Pavement data

Performance data

Pavement performance data (Average Deflection, Load Transfer Efficiency and Visual Survey) for test sections being monitored under TxDOT Research project 0-6274 in Texas.

General information

General pavement information ( Construction Year, Slab Thickness, Location) and Plan Sets for Continuously Reinforced Pavements and Concrete Pavement Continuous Design in Texas.

Special & Experimental

General pavement information, Plan Sets, research findings and research reports for various Special and Experimental rigid pavement sections such as Bonded Overlay, Unbonded Overlay, Post Tension Concrete Pavement and Whitetopping pavements constructed by TxDOT.
